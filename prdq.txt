# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

# QuoteFlow Pro

### Intelligent Quotation Management Platform

Version: 1.0
Status: Draft
Product Type: Web Application
Target Release: Production Software

---

# 1. Executive Summary

QuoteFlow Pro is a modern web-based quotation management platform designed to eliminate the inefficiencies of manual quotation creation.

Organizations currently spend significant time preparing quotations manually, leading to:

* Formatting inconsistencies
* Alignment issues
* Human calculation errors
* Delayed client responses
* Difficulty managing historical quotations

The platform automates quotation generation, calculation, approval workflows, document management, and reporting while maintaining a premium enterprise-grade user experience.

---

# 2. Problem Statement

Current quotation creation processes rely heavily on spreadsheets, word processors, and manual calculations.

Pain Points:

* Time-consuming quotation preparation
* Frequent alignment and formatting issues
* Calculation mistakes
* Lack of standardized templates
* Poor version control
* Difficulty tracking quotation status
* Manual PDF creation
* Lack of centralized storage

The system aims to reduce quotation creation time by over 80% while ensuring accuracy and consistency.

---

# 3. Product Vision

Create the most efficient and visually premium quotation management platform that enables businesses to generate professional quotations within minutes instead of hours.
